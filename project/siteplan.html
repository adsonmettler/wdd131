Tourism Company Site Plan

Overview

Site Name

Canadian XP

  • This name represents the Canadian experience offered through tours guided by Canadian XP.

Site Purpose

This website development project focuses on creating a site for a hypothetical tourism company in Canada. The goal of the website is to showcase the trip packages the company offers and highlight the main famous tourist spots covered by the company in Canada.

  • The home page will feature the main tourist attractions and guided trip offers.
  • Visitors seeking more information about scheduling a guided trip will be directed to a page where they can fill out a form to submit an information request.
  • Additionally, there will be an extra page displaying more places, restaurants, and hotels that the company partners with, along with a filter feature to help users find specific information.

Scenarios

  • Target Customers: Adventurous individuals aged 16 to 60, passionate about outdoor sports, seeking nature-centric experiences in Alberta, Canada.
  • Desires: They want thrilling whitewater rafting adventures, safety assurance, and a community of like-minded enthusiasts.
  • Expectations: Accessible details on guided experiences, safety measures, and equipment quality to make informed decisions.
  • Unmet Needs: A seamless platform addressing safety concerns, offering tailored adventures, and fostering a vibrant community spirit.
  • Access Preference: Primarily mobile users, accessing the site on-the-go for quick and convenient booking and information retrieval.
